(lemon)grass: culture + food


the root of my inspiration for cooking comes from my mother (kuv niam). growing up, kuv niam was always cooking wholesome 'hmong' food. i didn't have the traditional american breakfast of cereal, pankcakes, and eggs. no, i had the hmong breakfast: cooked rice and eggs with ketchup and chili paste. maybe the occasional cereal, but that was at school. my mother not only cooked hmong food, but she loved learning new recipes and dishes. She had her tricks on how to cooked this and that. But the one thing that my mom always pay close attention to was her rice. rice was the foundation of every meal. rice must be cooked correctly or the meal will be ruined.
